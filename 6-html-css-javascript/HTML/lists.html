Kraft Mac and Cheese (not sponsored)

Ingredients

  • 6 cups of water
  • 1 pkg, Kraft Macaroni and Cheese Dinner
  • 1/4 milk butter, cut up
  • 1/4 cup milk

Directions

  1. Bring water to boil in medium saucepan. Add Macaroni; cook 7 to 8 min. or until tender, stirring occasionally.
  2. Drain. (Do not rinse.) Return macaroni to pan.
  3. Add butter, milk and Cheese Sauce Mix; mix well.
